faqs: 
  - question: "Why do I need a website if I already have Google Maps and Instagram?"
    answer: "Google Maps and Instagram help people discover you. A website helps them choose you. It explains what you do, shows your services and pricing, adds proof through reviews and cases, and answers the questions people have before they contact you. Most importantly, it guides them to one clear action: book, call, or WhatsApp."

  - question: "How does a website help me get more clients?"
    answer: "A good website is a sales flow. It turns attention into trust, and trust into inquiries. It works by making your offer clear, showing proof and results, removing doubts with details and FAQ, and making contact effortless. When it is structured well, a visitor understands in seconds what you do, why you are different, and what to do next."

  - question: "What if my business already gets clients without a website?"
    answer: "That is great. A website does not replace what already works, it multiplies it. It increases conversion from the traffic you already have and helps you win clients who compare options before choosing. It also protects you, because platforms and algorithms change. A website is your owned channel and your most controllable asset."
  
  - question: "Can I use a link-in-bio page instead of a full website?"
    answer: "A link page can be useful, but it is not designed to convert. It does not rank well on Google and it rarely explains your services in a convincing way. A website is where you build credibility, show proof, and create a clear path to booking. Think of a link page as a directory, and a website as your main sales page."

  - question: "Will my website help me show up on Google?"
    answer: "Yes, if it is built on the right foundation. Structure, speed, mobile experience, and on-page SEO matter. A website also lets you publish content that answers real customer questions. Over time, this creates visibility in search and brings qualified traffic that is already looking for your service."

  - question: "How fast can you launch a website?"
    answer: "Most websites go live in 7 to 14 days, depending on scope and how quickly we receive content like photos, services, and reviews. We keep the process simple and focused, so you get a strong result without months of back and forth."
  
  - question: "What do you need from me to start?"
    answer: "A short list of services, your WhatsApp number, your logo (if you have one), photos, and a few reviews. If you do not have everything, we can still start and help you structure the content step by step."
  
  - question: "Is the website mobile friendly and fast?"
    answer: "Yes. Mobile and speed are priorities. Most visitors come from a phone, and they leave if the site feels slow. We build for clarity, fast loading, and easy contact, especially through WhatsApp."

  - question: "Do you include SEO?"
    answer: "Every project includes a strong SEO baseline: clean page structure, proper titles and metadata, and technical basics. If you want growth, we can also run ongoing SEO through content and optimization."

  - question: "Do you offer support after launch?"
    answer: "Yes. We offer a monthly care plan for updates, improvements, content help, and performance monitoring. This keeps your website sharp and reliable as your business evolves."
